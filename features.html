Designed for warmth

Get warmer with Warma.

Stitched together

This hoodie is stitched to additional components to provide extra warmth and comfort. The neck is joined to a snood, which is attached to a mask. This can be worn in a variety of ways. The sleeves are joined to thumb loops.

Snood

The snood is another key feature of this hoodie. It can be used as an alternative to wearing the mask while your neck is covered. It is attached to the hoodie and mask so it securely prevents cold wind, rain or snow from entering inside.

Thumb-loops

The thumb-loops can serve as gloves, or as attachment to wear when gloves are worn. They make the sleeves immovable, preventing skin from being exposed to cold environments. Thumb loops do not have to be worn as they easily fold within the sleeves.
